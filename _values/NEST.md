<p>NEST – Nurturing Every Student Together, is a brand-new video series specially
created for parents. Through NEST, we aim to keep you updated on school
matters while providing practical strategies and useful insights on how
we can work together to support our children’s growth and well-being.</p>
<p></p>

<p>In this episode of our Parenting for Wellness series, we explore the importance
of supporting our children’s mental health. The video shares how the school
nurtures students’ emotional wellbeing through Social-Emotional Learning
and a supportive school environment, while also highlighting how parents
can play an important role at home. Together, school and home can create
a strong support system that helps our children grow into resilient and
emotionally healthy individuals.</p>
<p></p>

<p>In this first episode, we are featuring a “nest of resources” that includes
Grow Well SG and Parenting for Wellness. These platforms offer a rich collection
of parenting tips, strategies, and ideas that can help cultivate positive
habits in our children so they can grow up healthy, confident, and resilient.</p>
