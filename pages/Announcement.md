<p><strong>Greetings, Alumni!</strong>
</p>
<p>We are thrilled to announce our <strong>2026 Alumni Day</strong>, bringing
together former students who graduated from West Spring Primary for a heartwarming
reunion on 2 September 2026.</p>
<p>This year's theme, "Becoming," celebrates the unique journey of every
West Springer. Each chapter of our lives brings new experiences, challenges
and opportunities that shape who we are, yet we are united by the place
where our journey first began.</p>
<p>As you return to familiar grounds, we invite you to reconnect with the
people who have been part of your story, reflect on your growth, and look
ahead with hope and purpose. After all, becoming is not about reaching
a destination – it is about embracing the journey and continuing to write
your story.</p>
<p>Interested WSPS Alumni should register their interest via the following
link. <a href="https://go.gov.sg/wspsalumni26" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/wspsalumni26</a>.</p>
<p>Please note that the school <strong><u>will not</u></strong> be open for
Alumni to visit during Teachers’ Day on 3 September as teachers will be
engaged in Teachers’ Day activities.</p>
<p>We look forward to welcoming you back to West Spring on 2 September!</p>
