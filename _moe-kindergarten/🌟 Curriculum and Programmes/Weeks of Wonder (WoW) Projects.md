<h1>Collaborative, self-directed learning</h1>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/wonder.png">
</div>
<p>Each term at MOE Kindergarten (MK) culminates in a <strong>Weeks of Wonder (WoW) project</strong>.
In a year, children complete 2 WoW projects in English and another 2 in
their Mother Tongue Language (MTL). Children will work together with their
friends on a topic of their choice.</p>
<p>Through WoW projects, children learn how to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Make their own decisions.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop problem-solving, process and investigative skills.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Collaborate with one another.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Communicate effectively in English and their MTL.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>Through the process of finding answers to their own questions, WoW projects
allow children to:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Experience the joy of self-motivated learning.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Build their confidence.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Strengthen their learning dispositions or positive behaviours and attitudes
towards learning.</p>
</li>
</ul>
